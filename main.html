Seventeen Unit

Vocal Team is a five member sub-unit of Seventeen under Pledis Entertainment. The group consists of Woozi, Jeonghan, Joshua, DK, and Seungkwan.

Hip-Hop Team is a four member sub-unit of Seventeen under Pledis Entertainment. The group consists of S.Coups, Wonwoo, Mingyu, and Vernon.

Performance Team is a four member Sub-Unit of Seventeen under Pledis Entertainment. The group consists of Hoshi, Jun, The8, and Dino.
